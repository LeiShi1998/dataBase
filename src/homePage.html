  • Overview

    The Selenoprotein Database is a website for storing the data of the selenoprotein of eukaryotic organisms. At present, the website has imported the predicted selenoprotein information from the article (article name), including species, family, nucleic acid sequence, amino acid sequence, secis sequence, selenoprotein gene structure diagram and other information.In addition to the detailed information of a single selenoprotein, statistical analysis of species and protein families were carried out.The selenoprotein database can also be queried by blast.

  • Submitting to SPDB

    Submit the data to the Selenoprotein Database, you can display your selenoprotein data more freely, and get new findings through the increasing data.You can send data to the administrator by email.
